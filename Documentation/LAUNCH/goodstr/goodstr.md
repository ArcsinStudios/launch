# `goodstr`

## Functions
<small>Defined in `goodstr_impl.h`</small>
|Function|Description|
|---|---|
|`split`|splits `orig` with delimiter `delim`|
|`concat`|concatenates `list` with separator `sep`|
|`replace`|replaces `from`s in `orig` with `to`|
|`replace_inplace`|in-place version of `replace`|
|`ltrim`|removes whitespaces on the left of `orig`|
|`rtrim`|removes whitespaces on the right of `orig`|
|`trim`|removes whitespaces on both sides of `orig`|
|`ltrim_inplace`|in-place version of `ltrim`|
|`rtrim_inplace`|in-place version of `rtrim`|
|`trim_inplace`|in-place version of `trim`|
